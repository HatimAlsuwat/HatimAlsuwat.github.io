Course Overview

This course provides a detailed introduction to data protection and privacy law. Also, will explore emerging privacy and data protection issues. Students will be challenged to critically examine how e.g. personal, financial, health and transactional data are managed and who has access to this information. It will require students to assess emerging legal, regulatory, data protection and personal privacy issues raised by widespread access to personal information, including genetic data. Particular attention is given to data protection regulations and personal data protection standards in Saudi Arabia.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, students should be able to:
  • Describe the concepts and principles of data protection and privacy.
  • State the difference between preserving privacy and protecting data in terms of regulations, techniques, and approaches.
  • Compare the different approaches to data protection regulations in Saudi Arabia.
  • Demonstrate the knowledge of tools and techniques that improves privacy protection.
  • Discuss the impact of national and international privacy legislation and regulations on the work of organizations and individuals.
