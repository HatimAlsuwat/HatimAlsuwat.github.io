Course Overview

This is a second course in discrete mathematics. This course provides more foundations and skills that students need in their further study of computer science and Engineering. These topics include Number theory, Counting, Relations, Graphs, and Trees

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, students will be able to:
  • Identify a problem and analyze it in terms of its significant parts and the information needed to solve it
  • Apply appropriate mathematical and statistical concepts and operations to interpret data and to solve problems.
  • Formulate and evaluate possible solutions to problems and select and defend the chosen solutions.
  • Communicate the solutions of technical problems to other professionals.
